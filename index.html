Bug log:

1. Description of the bug:

Basically if you open the website on mobile,for some reason it'll be a bit zoomed in so you have to zoom out with your fingers to see this in all of it's full glory

Update:This occurred after June 13th and I don’t have an exact date since I was writing this as of June 18th. That bug has made a return since I had to change the tablet screen's dimensions from dynamic to static.

Static means that the dimensions like height,width,length are locked in and that their values won’t change.

Dynamic means that depending on the content in the box,the height,width,and length would change to fit the content in the box.
